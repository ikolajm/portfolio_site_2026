Design System

“I’m doing X later—feel free to join!”

Spontaneous planning used to be easy. You’d run into someone, float an idea, and suddenly your night looked a little better. But when work, shopping, and social life all take place behind screens, those moments of frictionless connection start to disappear.

Making plans shouldn’t feel like project management. Yet modern social planning often comes with too many questions, too much back-and-forth, and just enough friction to make you give up.

Some of the best plans start with a quick run-in, a shared moment, or a mutual “sure, why not?”. This app makes it easy to float ideas, discover who’s free, and turn everyday moments into shared experiences. Less overthinking, more showing up—just like it used to be!
